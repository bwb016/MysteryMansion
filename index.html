About this game

The main antagonist lures you to this mansion where he says in this mansion you will find treasure,

however this is not the case. He lures you to in there to steal your soul. He is a sorcerer and he has

held a grudge with the protagonist since they were young. As such your mission is to escape the

mansion before he has the chance to steal your soul. In this mansion you will be able to level up your

character and obtain different weapons to fight the antagonist's boss like minions. There will be allies

there to help you in your quest to escape his clutches. At the end of the game you will fight the main

antagonist who is stronger than what you could be. The level cap can be set to 20 and the final boss

himself will be level 22. The allies you meet may give you weapons or power-ups some of these may

be kept others may be dismantled.
